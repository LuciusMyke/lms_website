About

KiddleLamp Learning Center

“The shining stars behind Kiddielamp Learning Center Mr. Larry S. Sindac and Ms. Rizabell Q. Mamugay, guiding every little dream to shine bright!”

Kiddielamp Learning Center began as a simple dream a place where young learners could discover the joy of learning in a nurturing and playful environment. With patience, love, and dedication, that dream slowly grew into a vibrant community filled with laughter, creativity, and curiosity.

Through years of hard work and faith, the center blossomed into a haven where children are encouraged to explore, imagine, and believe in themselves. Every corner of the school reflects the care and passion poured into building a space where learning feels like an adventure and every child feels valued.

Kiddielamp continues to shine as a symbol of growth, hope, and dedication. What started as a small vision has now become a strong foundation for future learner proving that with heart, perseverance, and purpose, even the simplest dreams can grow into something truly beautiful.
